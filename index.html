About Us

The main purpose of the project is to provide Students a platform to get their problem resolve at college level. For this purpose we will be making a Web based application for our university. The application will contain certain features which will be going to benefit a student in many aspects

  • Event/News update
  • E-Learning portal
  • Notes Section
  • Books Sharing and selling
  • Faculty contact and mail

The DITU Web based Application will contains the features which are going to benefit college students during their semester and placement time as well.

About SPECIAL FEATURES

  • 01Job Openings and Query Related to it

    Inside this we will contact with our alumni who are working in present companies and who are doing jobs at any place. According to them, we will make chapters like Delhi chapter, Bangalore chapter etc, and then the last year students will get all the information regarding jobs. And our alumni will put all the information and any job that came out in their companies and its requirements. So in this way anyone can take job according to their interest.

  • 02Event/News update

    In this section all news and upcoming events related to university and universityâ€™s club can be found.

  • 03Notes Section

    Here we will get all the notes and question paper of any subject, and of any year and anyone can post it. We will also put the course structure in it so that no one has any problem at the end time and important links related to that course also.

  • 04Books Sharing and selling

    In this section anyone can put his book which he or she is willing to share or sell and the one who want book can contact with the sharer or seller.

  • 05E-Learning Platform

    In this platform, both faculty, as well as a student, can log in and teachers can upload different assignments and courses and assign those courses to students.
